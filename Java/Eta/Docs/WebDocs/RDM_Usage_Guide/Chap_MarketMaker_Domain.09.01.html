The Market Maker domain provides access to market maker quotes and trade information. The list of market makers is sent in the form of an Map. Each entry represents one market maker (using that market maker’s Id as its key) and contains an FieldList describing information such as that market maker’s bid and ask prices, quote time, and market source.
 
Note: GenericMsg(s) are not supported for the MARKET_MAKER Reuters Domain Model.
